I wanted to make Libre Spiritus as I noticed there was a lack of film production programs around the Bay Area. So long as there was a place for freelance filmmakers to meet, film can be made.
I realized that I wanted not just a network but a real life platform as well where people can collaborate on projects together.
I created Libre Spiritus in 2013 at Berkeley and found all the students who were interested in film to join and start working on the platform and network.
Libre Spiritus was realized and it became much more than just a platform. I had created a think tank for film production.
The connections started growing from UC Berkeley to many other schools in the Bay Area from SFSU to SJSU.
The organization kept growing and now we're at 150 members in the Bay Area making it a huge step for us.
The Philosophy of Libre Spiritus is to "free your soul." We don't want anything in the way of your film idea being realized and produced.
We wish to provide everyone with the opportunity to produce great quality film which has not been produced before in Hollywood and other mainstream areas due to market concerns. We want people to be risky and hungry for new ideas.
At 150 members we are now looking for a way to make our mission virtual and bring the world's filmmakers together.
